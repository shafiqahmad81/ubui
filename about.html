About Us

Founded in 2024, UBUI emerged from the hope and unity of the historic Mass Uprising and is dedicated to strengthening US-Bangladesh relations through democracy, religious freedom, and economic independence. Guided by a diverse, high-impact board of advisors and directors, UBUI fosters strategic partnerships across energy security, climate change, IT & innovation, healthcare, education, and socio-economic progress.

By leveraging US investments, expertise, and technology, we drive renewable energy, digital transformation, and sustainable development. UBUI invites professionals, entrepreneurs, academics, and policymakers to collaborate, invest, and innovate. Together, we can build a thriving Bangladesh, powered by resilient infrastructure, a vibrant economy, and empowered communities.
