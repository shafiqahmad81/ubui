Mission

Creating meaningful partnerships and fostering growth, that’s what we are focusing on. This will prove to be beneficial for both Bangladesh and the United States. Here are the main pillars of our approach:

  1. Empowering Bangladesh’s Economy.
  2. Strengthening Bilateral Ties.
  3. Fostering Innovation and Sustainability.
  4. Working Toward Shared Aspirations.

Vision

To build a prosperous and stable region, through the collaboration between Bangladesh and the United States. Here are the main pillars of our approach:

  1. Strengthening Partnerships.
  2. Promoting Human Rights and Inclusivity.
  3. Empowering Future Leaders.
  4. Driving Sustainable Progress.
